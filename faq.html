I got this idea from the Simply Nailogical FAQ, I borrowed some questions and sarcasm from her superior blog site.

  1. Favorite nail polish?
    • Essie's No Place Like Chrome. I love ~all~ multichrome and holo polishes, too. I love all nail polishes people give me for free. I hate the smell of Poparazzi opaque car paint nail polish.

  2. Favorite top coat?
    • I don't have one, oops. I've been using Sally Hansen's Dries Instantly top coat and it hasn't failed me yet. Then I use Revlon's Ultimate Shine Top Coat to make things extra super glossy wow.

  3. Favorite base coat?
    • I also don't have one, oops. Previously I used Revlon's Quick Dry Base Coat and then I bought Essies Primer when that bottle ran out. I will keep using them until I pay off my student loans.

  4. Favorite matte top coat?
    • Probably Essie's Matte About You? I don't do matte nails too often because I use glitter, the herpes of arts and crafts, on my nails.

  5. Favorite nail polish brand?
    • Cirque Colors is beautiful and I love it and they make my favorite black polish Memento Mori.

  6. Where do you get your nail polishes?
    • Either CVS (my second favorite place in the entire worlds behind WalMart because I'm a Taurus and materialism consumes me) for the bulk of my regular nail polishes or Live Love Polish for the nail polishes that blow my mind andalso wallet.
  1. What do you take pictures and make videos with?
    • Before August 2018, I used a Canon PowerShot S3 that was made in 2007. My dad got it at Goodwill for $15 dollars. It gets the job done when it's four AA batteries don't run out. Now that I have a Real Job, I use the Canon Rebel T6 with an 85mm off brand lens.

  2. What kind of lighting do you use?
    • This really cheap lighting kit from Amazon. It does, in fact, produce light. I don't use a soft box because I want the light to reflect the sparkles in my nail polish.

  3. Where do you get your backgrounds?
    • The clearance section of my local Michael's craft store.

  4. What do you edit with?
    • I run a dual boot Windows/Linux, and since Linux hasn't caught up to the nail art video editing world and I must use Windows, I paid money for the Movavi Video Editor and I did not pay money for Fotor and paint.net. (Yes I've tried GIMP, yes I prefer Windows Paint). I get my music for the two videos I made once off the public domain world like everyone else who can't license things.

  1. How many nail polish bottles do you have?

    • It used to be dianailsit, but then someone misread it as Diana Is Lit so I decided never again. My dear squadfam friend gave me the name Undenailable. Trademark coming soon.
